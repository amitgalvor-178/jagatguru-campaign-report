Learnings & Insights
6 things that worked · 6 that didn't · 4 audience signals
⌄
What Worked
Retargeting was the single highest-ROI lever in the campaign. Running testimonial retargeting ads after the awareness phase drove 31 of 38 total leads at ₹92 CPL — more than 4× better than the ₹150–₹400 EdTech benchmark. YouTube Ads built the audience pool; Meta retargeting converted them. The two-touch funnel is validated.
LearningEvidenceSignal
Retargeting multiplied lead volume 31 of 38 leads from retargeting at ₹92 CPL Scale this
Creator content massively outperformed brand content 4.5× avg IG views/post; Dhurga 1.14L combined views at ₹0.02/view Scale this
Alumni / peer testimonials drove best paid CTR Past Student 2: 11.06% CTR, ₹0.25 CPC — strongest of all Google creatives Make more
Focusing on Chennai (Dhurga) outperformed broad TN targeting Dhurga's Chennai-first content: 1,03,610 YT views at 8.94% CTR; highest lead density from Chennai Sharpen geo
English content attracted a more urban, relevant audience than Tamil Higher completion rates and stronger lead-to-click conversion on English creatives vs Tamil-language ads Lead with English
Instagram built from zero showed early traction 0 → 54 followers; 65K views; top Reel 13,251 views — all in 6 weeks from a blank account Keep building
What Didn't Work
Meta leads came in but quality was low. YouTube Ads drove 89% of ad spend with only 7 attributed leads. High click volumes (1,13,842 Google clicks + 7,642 Meta clicks) didn't translate proportionally to form completions — pointing to either landing page friction or a broad audience mismatch. YT's role was awareness and pool-building, not direct conversion.
LearningEvidenceSignal
Meta lead quality was poor High link clicks (7,642), low verified form completions — many enquiries unqualified Qualify harder
YouTube Ads didn't generate direct leads ₹47,840 spend · 7 attributed leads · implied ₹6,834 CPL on YT alone Awareness role only
Awareness Founder video significantly underperformed 1.12% CTR vs 8–11% for alumni; ₹4.94 CPC vs ₹0.25–₹0.42; ₹7,577 spent, 0 conversions Cut or rework
UAE market investment did not convert ₹27,083 total (₹2,083 ads + ₹25,000 TamilMom fee) · 0 leads attributed Validate demand first
0 admissions closed from 38 leads Funnel drops at sales / follow-up stage — not a media problem, a conversion process gap Sales process fix
